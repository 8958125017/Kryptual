CROWDSALE SETUP

The most important and exciting part of the crowdsale process. Here you can define parameters of your crowdsale campaign.

Tier is required
Name of a tier. e.g. PrePreIco PreICO, ICO with bonus A, ICO with bonus B. etc. We simplified that and will increment a number after each tier.
Wallet Address is required
Where the money goes after investors transactions. Immediately after each transaction. We recommend to setup a multisig wallet with hardware based signers.
Start Time is required
Date and time when the tier starts. Can't be In the past from the current moment.
End Time is required
Date and time when the tier ends. Can be only in the future.
Rate is required Rate should be between 1-10000000000
Exchange rate Ethereum to Tokens. If it's 100, then for 1 Ether you can buy 100 token
Supply is required Supply should be between 1-10000000000
How many tokens will be sold on this tier. Cap of crowdsale equals to sum of supply of all tiers
